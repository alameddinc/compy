Welcome to Compy

Highlight & note anything on the web, capture and mark up screenshots, then hand your AI a clean, versioned task list.

🔒 100% local · no account · your data stays on your device

1 · Highlight anything

Select text on any page — a toolbar pops up. Pick a color; each color is a tag (Idea, Bug, For AI…). Hover a color to see its label. Alt+⇧+H

2 · Add a note

Click the Note button, or click any highlight later, to jot context. Or drop a page note not tied to any text. Alt+⇧+M

3 · Screenshot & annotate

Capture the visible page, draw boxes / arrows / text, then download, copy, or Save to your on-device gallery. Alt+⇧+S

4 · Copy for AI

One click copies a versioned Markdown task list (note + quote + URL + #tag), grouped by site. Paste it to your AI agent — it sees exactly what to do.

5 · Done → Archive

Mark a note (or a whole selection) done — it leaves the board and future exports, and lands in the Archive. Restore anytime.
